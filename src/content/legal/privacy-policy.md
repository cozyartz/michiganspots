## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information**:
- Reddit username (via OAuth)
- Email address (optional, for updates and notifications)
- Display name and profile information
- Password (if you create a native account)

**Partnership & Payment Information**:
- Business name and contact details (for partners)

- Spot discoveries and submissions
- Photos and descriptions

- Community interactions

**Communications**:
- Support inquiries and correspondence
- Survey responses
- Feedback and suggestions

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data**:
- Pages visited and features used
- Time and date of visits
- Referring website or app
- Device type and operating system
- Browser type and version
- IP address and geolocation (approximate)
- Session duration and activity patterns

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies**:
- Session cookies (authentication via Lucia)
- Analytics cookies (Cloudflare Analytics)
- Preference cookies (settings and customization)
- See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies) for details

**Location Data**:
- Approximate location based on IP address (unless you opt in to precise location)
- Geolocation data when you submit spot discoveries (if you grant permission)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

**Reddit OAuth Integration**:
- Reddit username and public profile information
- Email address (if shared by Reddit)
- Reddit karma and account age
- Subreddit memberships (with your permission)

**Payment Processors**:
- Transaction data from Stripe
- Payment method information (tokenized)
- Billing history and invoice details

**Error Tracking**:
- Stack traces and error logs from Sentry
- Browser information and user actions leading to errors
- (Sentry data is anonymized where possible)

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

**Account Data**: Retained while your account is active, plus 90 days after deletion
**Payment Records**: Retained for 7 years (tax and accounting requirements)
**User Content**: Retained indefinitely unless you request deletion
**Analytics Data**: Aggregated and anonymized after 24 months
**Error Logs**: Retained for 90 days (Sentry)
**Marketing Data**: Until you opt out or request deletion

### 6.1 Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing privacy@statespots.com. We will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
