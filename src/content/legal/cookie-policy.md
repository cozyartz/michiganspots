## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences, login status, and activity.

### Types of Cookies:

**Session Cookies**: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
**Persistent Cookies**: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
**First-Party Cookies**: Set by State Spots directly
**Third-Party Cookies**: Set by our service providers (e.g., Cloudflare, Stripe, Sentry)

## 3. How We Use Cookies

State Spots uses cookies for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Essential Cookies (Cannot Be Disabled)

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly. Without them, you cannot use State Spots.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `auth_session` | Maintains your login session (Lucia authentication) | Session | First-party |
| `csrf_token` | Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks | Session | First-party |
| `__cf_bm` | Cloudflare bot management and security | 30 minutes | Third-party |

**Legal Basis**: Necessary for contract performance (providing the Service)

### 3.2 Analytics Cookies (Can Be Disabled)

These cookies help us understand how you use State Spots so we can improve the platform.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `_cfa` | Cloudflare Analytics - tracks page views and usage | 24 hours | Third-party |
| `_cfuvid` | Cloudflare Analytics - unique visitor identification | Session | Third-party |
| `analytics_session` | State Spots internal analytics | 1 year | First-party |

**Data Collected**:
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring website
- Device type and browser
- Approximate geolocation (IP-based)

**Legal Basis**: Legitimate interest (improving our Service)

**Opt-Out**: Use our cookie consent banner or browser settings

### 3.3 Preference Cookies (Can Be Disabled)

These cookies remember your settings and preferences to enhance your experience.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `theme` | Remembers your dark mode / light mode preference | 1 year | First-party |
| `language` | Stores your language preference | 1 year | First-party |
| `cookie_consent` | Remembers your cookie consent choices | 1 year | First-party |

**Legal Basis**: Consent (you can withdraw consent at any time)

### 3.4 Third-Party Cookies

We use trusted third-party services that may set their own cookies:

**Cloudflare** (Infrastructure & Security):
- `__cfduid`: Identifies trusted traffic (security)
- `__cf_bm`: Bot detection and security
- `_cfa`: Analytics (if enabled)

**Stripe** (Payment Processing):
- `__stripe_mid`: Fraud prevention and payment processing
- `__stripe_sid`: Session management for payment forms
- (Only set when you use payment features)

**Sentry** (Error Tracking):
- `sentry-sc`: Stores session context for error debugging
- (Only set when errors occur)

**Reddit OAuth** (Authentication):
- `reddit_session`: Maintains Reddit login state during OAuth
- `token_v2`: Reddit authentication token
- (Only set when you log in via Reddit)

## 6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### 6.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit State Spots, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:
- **Accept All**: Allow all cookies (essential, analytics, and preferences)
- **Reject Non-Essential**: Allow only essential cookies
- **Customize**: Choose which types of cookies to enable

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

### 6.2 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

**Google Chrome**:
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose "Block all cookies" or "Block third-party cookies"

**Mozilla Firefox**:
1. Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Choose "Delete cookies and site data when Firefox is closed"

**Safari**:
1. Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
2. Choose "Block all cookies"

**Microsoft Edge**:
1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and data stored
2. Choose "Block all cookies" or manage exceptions

**Note**: Blocking essential cookies will prevent you from using State Spots.

### 6.3 Third-Party Opt-Outs

**Cloudflare Analytics**: [Opt-out page](https://www.cloudflare.com/analytics-opt-out/)
**Stripe**: Stripe cookies are necessary for payment processing and cannot be disabled
**Sentry**: Errors are tracked only when they occur; you can disable JavaScript to prevent Sentry tracking (not recommended)

### 6.4 Do Not Track (DNT)

State Spots respects the "Do Not Track" browser signal. If DNT is enabled, we will:
- Disable analytics cookies
- Minimize data collection
- Not share data with third parties (except essential service providers)

## 13. Cookie Glossary

**Cookie**: Small text file stored on your device by a website
**Session**: The period during which you actively use the website
**Persistent Cookie**: Cookie that remains after you close your browser
**First-Party Cookie**: Cookie set by the website you're visiting
**Third-Party Cookie**: Cookie set by another domain (e.g., analytics provider)
**httpOnly**: Cookie flag that prevents JavaScript from accessing the cookie (security measure)
**Secure Flag**: Cookie flag that ensures transmission over HTTPS only
**SameSite**: Cookie attribute that restricts cross-site cookie sending
