## APPENDIX A: CLA Bot Integration

For automated CLA acceptance via GitHub, we use [CLA Assistant](https://cla-assistant.io) or similar.

**How it works**:
1. You submit a pull request
2. CLA bot comments asking for agreement
3. You reply with a comment: "I have read the CLA and I hereby sign the CLA"
4. Bot marks Your CLA as accepted
5. Maintainers can review and merge Your contribution

**Stored Records**:
- Your GitHub username and email
- Date and time of acceptance
- Pull request or commit that triggered acceptance
- IP address (for verification purposes)
